Recipe of fruit salad

  • by LENA MOLIN
  • 3 min read

Fruit salads are a great way to eat. Thus, you can independently come up with the recipes that fruit that you currently have. If you pour all this whipped sour cream with sugar, you get a real masterpiece.

  • SERVES
    2 as a main, 4 as a side
  • PREP TIME
    18 minute
  • Cook TIME
    15 minute
Ingredients
  • Ingredients
  • strawberries;
  • drain;
  • nectarines
  • peaches;
  • currant;
  • apricots;
  • lemon juice;
  • sour cream (200 grams);
  • sugar (100 grams);
  • dark chocolate for decoration.
Method of preparation
  • 1. The number of fruits may vary depending on your taste preferences.
  • 2. Rinse the fruit, drain the water.
  • 3. Chop them into pieces. Do not make them too small because they will quickly drain.
  • 4. Blend sour cream with sugar. Add a little lemon juice.
  • 5. Pour the mixture over the fruit. Leave a few slices for surface decoration. Mix.
  • 6. On a grater grate the chocolate. Decorate salad deferred fruit and chocolate.
  • 7. Bon appetit!

The all fruits, whether red, green, yellow, or black, offers so many possibilities to create an exciting lunch.

MEIKE PETERS
